Inspired by the idea of helping children with disabilities, we decided to help the Prague Center for Development of Autistic Children www.aba-centrum.cz raise money for a Owl's House, a sensory dynamic training equipment with all necessary facilities that promote a sensomotor integration to give the children a greater opportunity for their future wellbeing. Join us and contribute to the future of these children – in our future! Together we can change somebody's life and make the world a better place!
